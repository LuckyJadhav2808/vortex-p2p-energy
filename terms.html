2. Platform Description

Vortex is a peer-to-peer solar energy trading platform that enables prosumers (solar panel owners) and consumers to trade solar energy directly through a bid-based matching system. The Platform operates using virtual credits for demonstration purposes.

4. Trading Rules

  • All bids placed on the Platform are binding and expire after 10 minutes.
  • The matching engine automatically pairs buy and sell bids based on price compatibility.
  • The clearing price is calculated as the midpoint between the buy and sell prices.
  • A platform fee of 2% is applied to each trade and deducted from the seller's credit.
  • Users must maintain sufficient wallet balance to execute buy bids.
  • Prosumers must have sufficient energy balance to execute sell bids.
