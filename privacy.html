Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register on Vortex, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, zone/neighborhood, role (prosumer/consumer), and solar panel capacity.
  • Trading Data: Bid history, trade records, wallet transactions, and energy balances.
  • Usage Data: Page visits, feature interactions, and timestamps for platform activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate energy trading and bid matching between users.
  • To maintain wallet balances and transaction records.
  • To calculate CO₂ savings and green impact metrics.
  • To generate analytics and price suggestions based on trading patterns.
  • To provide administrative tools for platform monitoring.
  • To send notifications about trades, bid expiries, and platform updates.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Realtime Database. Firebase employs industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Data is stored in Google Cloud data centers and is subject to Google's security practices.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the safety and security of the Platform and its users.
  • Anonymized trading data may be used for market analytics visible to all users.
  • Zone and trade activity data (without personal identifiers) is shared for market transparency.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your profile, trade history, and wallet transactions at any time.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information through the Profile page.
  • Export: You can export your transaction history as CSV or PDF from the Wallet page.
  • Deletion: Contact the admin to request account deletion.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Vortex uses Firebase Authentication which stores session tokens in browser local storage. No third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels are used.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the Vortex platform administrators.
